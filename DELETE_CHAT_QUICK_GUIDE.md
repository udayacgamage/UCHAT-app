# ⚡ DELETE CHAT - QUICK GUIDE

## 🎯 HOW TO DELETE A CHAT

### Step 1: Open Chat List
- Go to Home screen
- Make sure you're on "Chats" tab

### Step 2: Swipe Left
- 👆 Swipe from right to left on any chat
- Red delete button appears

### Step 3: Chat Deleted
- ✅ Chat is deleted immediately
- 📱 "Chat deleted" message shows

---

## 🎯 HOW TO DELETE A GROUP

### Step 1: Open Group List
- Go to Home screen
- Switch to "Groups" tab

### Step 2: Swipe Left
- 👆 Swipe from right to left on any group
- Red delete button appears

### Step 3: Group Deleted
- ✅ Group is deleted immediately
- 📱 "Group deleted" message shows
